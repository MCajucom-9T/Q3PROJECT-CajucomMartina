How to reload it?

On-site

  1. Locate a Reloading Station – Visit a beep™ reloading kiosk, LRT/MRT stations, partner convenience stores (like 7-Eleven), or select retail outlets.
  2. Inform the Cashier or Machine – Tell the cashier that you want to reload your beep™ card or follow the prompts on a self-service kiosk.
  3. Hand Over Your beep™ Card – If using a cashier-assisted reload, give them your card so they can process the transaction.
  4. Specify the Amount – Choose how much you want to load onto your beep™ card (typically in fixed denominations).
  5. Make the Payment – Pay the required amount using cash or other accepted payment methods.
  6. Confirm the Transaction – Wait for the transaction to be processed and ensure the correct amount is loaded.
  7. Retrieve Your beep™ Card – Take your card back and check the updated balance, either on the kiosk screen or receipt.

Online

  1. Download the beep™ App – Install the official beep™ app from the Google Play Store or Apple App Store.
  2. Open the App and Log In – Launch the app and sign in to your account, or create one if you’re a new user.
  3. Tap "Load Card" – Select the option to reload your beep™ card.
  4. Enter Card Details – Input your beep™ card number or tap your NFC-enabled card on a supported phone.
  5. Select Load Amount – Choose the amount you want to add to your beep™ card balance.
  6. Choose a Payment Method – Pay using available options like credit/debit cards, e-wallets (GCash, Maya), or online banking.
  7. Confirm Payment – Review the transaction details and proceed with the payment.
  8. Perform Card Activation – If using an NFC-enabled phone, tap your beep™ card to activate the load. If not, visit a beep™ e-loading station to finalize the load.
  9. Check Updated Balance – Verify that the correct amount has been added to your beep™ card.
